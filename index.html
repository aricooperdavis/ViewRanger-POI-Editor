ViewRanger POI Editor

Bulk edit the POIs in your ViewRanger Objects Database

This site facilitates the editing of your ViewRanger POIs. Start by uploading your Viewranger Objects Database and the POIs will be loaded and displayed on the map. Then:

  • Mouseover a POI to see its name
  • Click a POI to toggle whether or not the POI will be included in the exported database.
  • Shift-click and drag to select multiple POIs to toggle at once
  • Reload the page to start over.
Once you've made your changes you can export your database and the map will be updated to display your new database. All of this is done in your browser using JavaScript, so you're not transferring your database files anywhere, which maximises your privacy and security. As such you'll need a modern browser to use this tool.


A free and open source project by Ari Cooper-Davis, using sql.js and leaflet.
